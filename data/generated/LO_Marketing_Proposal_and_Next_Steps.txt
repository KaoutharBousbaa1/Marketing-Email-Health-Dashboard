5)  Cold  Non-Buyer  Plan  (What  we  do  with  them)  
Cold  non-buyers  will  go  through  a  structured  3-step  process:  
Step  1:  Re-permission  (2  emails  max)  
Goal:  -  Confirm  they  want  to  hear  from  us  and  protect  deliverability.  
Mechanic:
 -  Email  1:  “Do  you  still  want  these  emails?”  (one  button:  “Yes,  keep  me  
subscribed”)
 -  Email  2  (48–72  hours  later):  “Last  call  to  stay”  (same  single  CTA)  Routing:  -  Clickers/repliers  =  re-warmed  →  enter  Step  2.  -  No  action  =  suppress  from  broadcasts  (remove  or  archive).  

=== PAGE 5 ===
Step  2:  Re-warm  via  the  free  learning  product  
Goal:  -  Rebuild  open  +  click  habit  using  daily  momentum.  -  Deliver  “14-Day  AI  Learning  Sprint”  with  short,  tangible  wins.   Potential  CTA:  -  “Click  to  get  tomorrow’s  prompt.”  
Step  3:  Present  the  Easy  Yes  product  (paid  
micro-product)
 
Timing:  -  After  7  days  of  engagement   Offer  frame:   -  “If  you  want  the  fastest  shortcut,  here’s  the  plug-and-play  pack.”   Goal:   -  Convert  warm  clicks  into  first  purchase,  then  route  into  workshop  promotions. 8)  Measurement  (What  we  track  weekly)  
List  health:  -  Open  rate  and  CTOR  by  segment  (not  just  overall).  -  Re-permission  click  rate  for  cold  segment.  -  %  of  the  cold  audience  moved  to  “re-warmed”  status.  Funnel:  -  Easy  Yes/Free  Product  conversion  rate  (cold  and  re-warmed).  -  Workshop  purchase  rate  by  segment.  -  Workshop  →  Bootcamp  conversion  rate  by  segment.  -  Time-to-purchase  from  lead  magnet  signup.  Deliverability  proxy:  -  Complaint  rate  and  unsubscribe  rate  by  segment  (watch  cold  re-entry  carefully).
